# Hi, I'm Anjali

I'm a **computational materials scientist** with a Ph.D. in Physics. I design and run atomistic simulations of metallic systems: bulk metallic glasses, liquid transition metals, and biodegradable implant alloys.

## What I work on

- **Classical molecular dynamics** in LAMMPS, on Mg-Ca and Al-Ca BMGs, liquid W and Mo, glass transition, transport phenomena, and structure-property analysis
- **Density functional theory** with Quantum ESPRESSO, for electronic structure, band structure, and total-energy calculations
- **Python pipelines** for batch MD post-processing (NumPy, SciPy, Pandas, dynasor)
- Currently extending into **machine-learning interatomic potentials** by fine-tuning MACE-MP-0 for Mg₂Ca

## Domain depth

Bulk metallic glasses, biodegradable Mg-Ca implant alloys, supercooled-liquid dynamics, phase-change materials, structure-property analysis.
